# Privacy Policy for Peckly

Last updated: February 2026

Peckly ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Peckly mobile application (the "App").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Peckly.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a) Information You Provide
- Reminder titles and list content
- Shared list data (if collaboration is used)
- Email address (only if account login is implemented)

### b) Automatically Collected Information
- Device type and operating system
- Anonymous usage data
- Crash reports and diagnostics

### c) Payments
All payments are processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play.
We do not collect or store your payment information.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and improve app functionality
- Enable reminder syncing (if cloud sync is enabled)
- Enable list sharing and collaboration
- Fix bugs and improve performance

We do not sell your personal data.

---

## 3. Data Storage

- Reminder data may be stored locally on your device.
- If cloud sync is enabled, data may be securely stored in cloud infrastructure.
- Shared lists require storing minimal collaboration metadata.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Peckly may use third-party services such as:

- RevenueCat (subscription management)
- Analytics providers (for anonymous usage insights)
- Cloud storage providers (for sync functionality)

These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service.
You may request deletion of your data at any time.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Access your data
- Request correction
- Request deletion
- Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at:

support@peckly.app

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Peckly is not directed toward children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Changes will be posted on this page.
